British Handmade Fabric Sofas

  • What's your idea of a sofa to come home to?

    After the cat or the dog, nothing can welcome you home as warmly as a sofa hand-picked from Sofas and Stuff. We feel emboldened to make this claim after hand-making 180,000 sofas in Britain over the past 30-odd years. At Sofas & Stuff, we have furniture-making coursing through our veins.

  • Shape? Stuffing? Fabric? Fibre? Colour?

    A sofa is a major purchase – and one you'll live with for a good long while. No pressure - we’d only urge you to take your time making up your mind.

  • Pay for British craftsmanship, not high street overheads

    We only take orders from this website or in one of our lovely old barns in the middle of nowhere. What we save in rents and rates we pass on to you.

  • Our Lifetime Guarantee - so comforting

    Despite our very reasonable prices, at the heart of a Sofas and Stuff sofa is a frame hand-crafted from properly seasoned timber that's been assembled by hand, glued and screwed so beautifully every frame comes with a Lifetime Guarantee. As does every spring.

  • Delivery in 4 - 6 weeks

    Whichever combination of personal preferences you decide on, our people in Nottingham and Preston will build, then deliver your sofa to your home in under six weeks. Find out more

  • Made-to-measure? It's an option

    If you have exceptionally long legs, a tricky bay window or want your sofa a bit higher in the back, say, opt for one of our 'made-to-measures'. It may cost a little more and take a little longer – but if it means you end up with a sofa to come home to, you may decide it's worth it.
