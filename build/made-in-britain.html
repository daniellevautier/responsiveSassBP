British made sofas

At Sofas & Stuff, we’re proud to say we make all our sofas, beds and mattresses to strict British standards, to order and by hand, in our workshops outside Nottingham.

We exert such quality control we're able to offer a lifetime guarantee on every model. The springs we favour are expertly tensioned for comfort and durability - and we’re generous with them (one of our king-sized mattresses contains a bouncy 1400).
